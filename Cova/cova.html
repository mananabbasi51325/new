We are Cova Psychology

Cova Psychology provides high quality, evidenced-based psychological assessment and treatment via face to face and telehealth sessions.

We offer evidence-based treatments including:

  • Cognitive Behaviour Therapy (CBT)
  • Mindfulness-based therapies
  • Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT)
  • Dialectical Behaviour Therapy (DBT)
  • Schema Therapy
  • Eye Movement Desensitisation Reprocessing (EMDR),EMDR Therapy Melbourne

We treat a wide range of psychological issues including:

  • Mood disorders such as Depression and Anxiety
  • Emotion dysregulation
  • Eating disorders and body image issues
  • Improving Relationships
  • Self-esteem and identity issues
  • Gender and Sexuality

Our Experience at Cova Psychology

Psychologists and Trainees

Cova Psychology offers clinical supervision by senior psychologists who are registered to provide supervision with the Australian Health Practitioner Regulation Agency (AHPRA). Supervision can be provided to psychologists, psychology students, psychology registrars, and mental health social workers. For more information about our supervisory services please contact us.

Doctors

Cova Psychology is passionate about developing collaborative relationships with doctors to ensure a high level of patient care. We endeavour to keep doctors up to date with concise information regarding the progress of their patients. In addition, we welcome input from doctors into the treatment of our patients. If you are a doctor and have questions about how Cova Psychology might be able to assist your patients, we welcome your enquiry, please contact us.
